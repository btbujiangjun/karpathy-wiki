A principled method for building general AI capabilities through curriculum learning over cross-entropy games. A meta-sampler generates games optimizing a meta-objective O that measures transfer value between games. The curriculum is built greedily by selecting games with maximal expected transfer to target capabilities.

## Key Characteristics

- Formal framework connecting game selection to capability transfer
- Transfer value as the central optimization quantity
- Principled meta-objective for curriculum construction
- Cross-entropy games provide a unified representation
