The idea that general AI capabilities can be built by growing a curriculum of cross-entropy games. The meta-sampler generates games that maximize transfer value between games, with the goal of producing broadly capable agents rather than specialists. Analogous to how humans develop general cognitive abilities through varied game-like experiences.

## Key Claims

- Transfer value is the central quantity for game selection
- A principled meta-objective can guide greedy curriculum building
- Games are a natural medium for cognitive training due to their compositional structure
