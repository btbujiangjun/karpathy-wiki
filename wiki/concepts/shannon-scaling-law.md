A unified theoretical framework modeling LLM training as information transmission over a noisy channel. Model parameters map to channel bandwidth; training tokens map to signal power. Reveals a fundamental Shannon capacity for LLMs, explaining non-monotonic phenomena like catastrophic overtraining and quantization degradation.
