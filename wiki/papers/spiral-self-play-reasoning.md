## Problem

LLMs lack robust reasoning capabilities that generalize beyond training distributions.

## Method

Self-play on zero-sum games to incentivize chain-of-thought reasoning. The competitive structure of zero-sum games naturally rewards deeper, more strategic thinking.

## Key Results

(Results detailed in Section 3.2 of the daily report — covers both reasoning and games domains.)

## Key Innovations

- Zero-sum game structure as natural reward shaping for reasoning
- Self-play generates increasingly difficult reasoning challenges
- Cross-domain transfer: game-trained reasoning generalizes to non-game tasks
